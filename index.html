THE MIGHTY FRIEND

{shati khan}

My name is Shati Khan and I’m a New York City-based transportation planner and aspiring plant lady. As a native New Yorker, I’ve always been passionate about urban systems and am excited to be working to improve the New York City transit landscape.

Beyond urban planning, my interests are many and varied. I’ve always had a “passion for fashion” (blech), love museums and traveling like most other millennials, and am always looking for a Korean language buddy!

Why The Mighty Friend?

The Mighty Friend is actually a misnomer! I came up with the name back in college when a friend told me that my last name, Khan, meant “mighty” (disclaimer: it does not). Combined with my first name, Shati, which actually means ‘friend’ in Bengali, we get The Mighty Friend!
